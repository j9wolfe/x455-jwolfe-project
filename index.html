Seas to Trees

What began as a family craving adventure turned into a journey through the ever-changing landscapes where ocean spray meets forest mist. With two kids in the backseat and a car packed with snacks, hiking boots, and way too many stuffed animals, we hit the road in search of something more—more wonder, more wild, more time together. From windswept beaches to towering redwoods, every stop offers a new chance to explore, climb, splash, and discover. Our route winds along the coast and deep into the trees, where curiosity leads the way and the map is only half the story. This blog is our trail journal—a mix of salty air, muddy boots, spontaneous detours, and wide-eyed moments we’ll never forget.

Our Coastal Crew

Family of four

Our journey follows the winding coastal highways, where ocean meets forest, and every turn offers something new to discover. This travelogue is a snapshot of our experience—breathtaking ocean views, misty forest hikes, epic sand dunes, and all the “wow” moments in between. We’re chasing fresh air, unforgettable sights, and the kind of memories that only come from hitting the open road together. Along the way, we’re sharing our favorite finds, tips for traveling with kids, and lots of photos to bring the trip to life. Whether you’re planning your own coastal adventure or just coming along for the ride, we’re glad you’re here!
